

 Color Contrast definition:

 Color Contrast is a ratio between adequate contrast between the foreground color and the 
 background color.

 Choosing colors with good contrast makes navigating, reading and interacting a nightmare.

 - Contrast problems are more common as we age
 
 - With good colors, websites and applications can be easier to use in more situations like
 In different lighting conditions

 WEB ACCESSIBILITY IS ESSENTIAL FOR SOME AND USEFULL FOR ALL.

 - NOT ALL ELEMENTS IN THE SCREEN NEED TO MEET THE COLOR REQUIREMENTS.
